About

Established in 1987, Al-Saba Restaurant has been a cornerstone of Hyderabad's culinary scene, offering an authentic blend of Mughlai, North Indian, and Chinese cuisines. Renowned for our signature dishes like Hyderabadi Biryani, Tandoori Chicken, and year-round Haleem, we pride ourselves on delivering flavorful meals in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional service ensures a memorable dining experience for every guest
