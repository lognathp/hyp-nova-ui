About

At Sri Kanya Parcels & Restaurant, we bring the rich flavors of Andhra cuisine to your table with a commitment to authenticity and quality. Whether you're dining in or ordering out, our goal is to provide a satisfying culinary experience that celebrates the vibrant tastes of South India.
