About

Established in 1942, Babai Hotel has been a cherished culinary landmark, renowned for its authentic South Indian flavors and timeless recipes. Originating from Vijayawada, our legacy has gracefully extended to Hyderabad, where we continue to serve our signature dishes like the ghee-laden Babai Idli and crispy Babai Dosa. With a commitment to quality and tradition, we invite you to experience the warmth of our hospitality and the richness of our cuisine, whether you're dining in or ordering online.
