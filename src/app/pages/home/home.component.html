Established in 2006, Mehfil Hyderabad has been a culinary beacon, offering authentic Hyderabadi flavors at pocket-friendly prices. Rooted in the rich legacy of the Grand Hotel since 1935, Mehfil has expanded to multiple locations, including Narayanguda, Attapur, Uddamgadda, Shaikpet, and Hitech City. Our diverse menu features signature biryanis, succulent kebabs, and a fusion of North Indian, Mughlai, and Chinese cuisines, ensuring a delightful dining experience for every palate. Whether you're dining in, taking away, or ordering online, Mehfil promises consistent quality and taste that resonates with food lovers across Hyderabad.
