About

Established in 2008 in Kukatpally, Chaitanya Food Court has become a cherished culinary destination in Hyderabad, offering a delightful fusion of South and North Indian cuisines. Our menu features signature dishes like Ulavacharu Chicken Biryani, Avakaya Mutton Biryani, and the ever-popular Sambar Rice, all crafted with authentic flavors and traditional recipes. Recognized as the "Best Andhra QSR of the Year" in 2023, we pride ourselves on delivering quality food that brings people together. Whether you're dining in or ordering online, experience the rich tapestry of Indian flavors that make every meal at Chaitanya Food Court a memorable one.
