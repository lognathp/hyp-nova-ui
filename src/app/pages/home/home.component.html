About

Established in 1997, Pista House has grown from a humble family venture into a global culinary destination, celebrated for preserving the authentic flavors of Hyderabadi cuisine. Our world-renowned Haleem, iconic Biryani, and a wide array of traditional delicacies are crafted with meticulous attention to detail, reflecting our commitment to quality and authenticity. With over 44 outlets worldwide, we continue to serve millions, offering a taste of tradition that resonates with food lovers across the globe.
