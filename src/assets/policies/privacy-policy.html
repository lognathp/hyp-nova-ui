PRIVACY POLICY FOR Arugu Kitchen

Service provided by HyperApps for Arugu Kitchen

1. Introduction

This ordering service (“Service”) for Arugu Kitchen is operated by HyperApps via arugu kitchen. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data for online food ordering.

2. Information We Collect

  • Mobile Number – For OTP login and WhatsApp alerts.
  • Name & Delivery Address – To process and fulfill your order.
  • Payment Information – Handled by RazorPay; we do not store card/bank details.
  • Technical Data – Analytics (IP, browser/device) via Google Analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Order verification and processing through OTP.
  • Payment handled securely by RazorPay.
  • Delivery coordination with assigned personnel.
  • WhatsApp alerts for order confirmations and updates.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • RazorPay: Payment processing (no data stored by us).
  • Delivery Team: Limited info for delivery only.
  • WhatsApp: For real-time order status alerts.
  • Legal Authorities: If required under applicable law.

5. Data Retention

  • Mobile numbers for receipts/order history.
  • Address retained with order records.
  • Payment data never stored (RazorPay handles records).

6. Security Measures

  • OTP-based logins.
  • PCI-DSS certified payment gateway (RazorPay).
  • All data transmissions secured via HTTPS.

7. Your Rights

  • Access or correct your personal data.
  • Request early deletion of your data post-fulfillment.
  • Opt out of WhatsApp alerts by replying "STOP".
  • Contact us via Section 9 for requests or concerns.

8. Third-Party Services

  • RazorPay: Secure payment gateway (see their privacy policy).
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage data tracking.
