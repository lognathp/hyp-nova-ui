🔁 Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025
Last Updated: May 21, 2025

At Shah Ghouse, we take great care in preparing and delivering your food. However, if you experience any issues with your order, we're here to help.

🔹 1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • You received the wrong item or an incomplete order.
  • Your food was not delivered.
  • The food was damaged or spoiled upon delivery.
  • Payment was charged more than once for the same order.

🔹 2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after the order is placed.
  • Delays caused by customer unavailability or incorrect address.
  • Dissatisfaction based on taste preference (unless quality is compromised).
  • Partial consumption or tampering of delivered food.

🔹 3. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us within 2 hours of delivery:

📧 prithvi@hyperapps.in

Please include:

  • Your order ID
  • Description of the issue
  • Photo evidence (if applicable)

🔹 4. Refund Processing

  • Once reviewed and approved, refunds will be initiated to your original payment method.
  • Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days.
  • For UPI or Razorpay payments, timelines may vary based on your bank.

🔹 5. Cancellations

Orders can only be canceled before food preparation begins. To cancel, contact us immediately via phone. If accepted, we’ll issue a full refund.
